Recruiting and hiring is a resource-intensive process, and finding the right talent for your various roles can be very challenging in the best of times!

Let Advantage Mississauga help you connect you to the right talent through a high-tech job-matching portal, Magnet.

Whether you are looking to fill one role or quickly build your team, Magnet is designed to help companies like yours efficiently and effectively source qualified candidates that meet your skills requirements, while lowering your recruiting costs.
 

Using a powerful technology platform and an automated candidate-matching technique, Magnet enables your job posting to actively search for only the candidates that match your requirements and that qualify for your opportunity.

Traditional job boards come at a great cost – between $400 and $700 per job for a 30-day posting – and do not always promise qualified candidates. With Magnet, employers can post a job for free. Magnet provides you with a list of pre-screened and pre-qualified candidates. If you wish to connect with an individual whose profile is a suitable match AND who has also expressed interest in that job, you can do so instantly and privately for only $9.99 per connection.
 

Pre-qualified candidates at your fingertips

Magnet connects you to thousands of prequalified candidates based on their skills, experience, and interests using a powerful job-matching technology platform.

Developed talent funnel

Magnet enables you to develop a network with prospective employees to accelerate hiring and drive productivity, allowing you to generate ‘pools’ of valuable relationships.

Private and secure hiring

Unlike many social media sites and job boards, Magnet protects individual privacy. The system is designed to limit potential hiring bias, as ‘connections’ between job seekers and employers are based on matching criteria.

Simplified job posting and hiring process

Magnet searches 24/7 for the best candidate matches and sends you screened lists. Avoid having to screen hundreds of resumes and unqualified responses, and only get introduced to qualified applicants.
